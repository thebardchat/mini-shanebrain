About

I'm a dispatch operator out of Alabama, a father of five, and a self-taught engineer building things most people say can't be done on a budget.

My daily driver is a Raspberry Pi 5 with 7.4 GB of usable RAM. No cloud GPU clusters. No venture capital. Just grit, late nights after the kids are asleep, and an obsession with making powerful technology accessible to ordinary people.

Every project I build — from AI orchestration to post-quantum security to literal space launch systems — serves one goal: leave something real behind for the next generation.

I believe the best software is built by people who actually need it. That's me. That's my family. That's the 800 million Windows users about to lose security updates.
